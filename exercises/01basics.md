## Logical operations

What is the result  (`true` or `false` or something else) of:

- `2 >= 2`
- `3 > 2 & 2 == 1`
- `3 > 2 | 2 == 1`
- `3 > 2 & 2 != 1`
- `!(3 > 2)`
- `!2 == 1`
- `3 > 2 & 2 = 1`
- `true & false & true`
- `true & false | true`
- `true & true | false`
- `true & (false | true)`
- `false & !false | true`
